Stacked Decks

Stacked Decks is a web Appliction made by myself and a small team at Boise Codeworks. We wanted to design an app that makes it easy and fun to design your own workouts, AKA decks, by filling it with cards. You can make your own deck, post your deck to the public, save others decks and make them your own. You can also track your progress per deck.

Keeper

Keeper was my final application at Boise CodeWorks. You can post pictures, AKA keeps, and save them into a album, AKA vaults. You can visits others accounts, vaults and keeps as well as make vaults private. This was built using a vue frontend and a C# backend with a SQL database, with dapper as a ORM. There 100 postman tests to insure proper privacy, protection, and quality

Towers

Tower was my first full-stack application. You are able to create events, buy tickets for events and comment on the events page. On your account page you are able to see what events you have created, cancel those events and dicard your ticket. This page has mostly mock data in it from postman tests. I used vue as a frontend framework and Node for the backend, along with mongoose and mongoDB.
